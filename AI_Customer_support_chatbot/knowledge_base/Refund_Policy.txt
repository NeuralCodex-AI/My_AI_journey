Refund Policy
TechNova Store offers a customer-friendly refund policy. Customers can request a refund within 30 days of receiving their order. Products must be returned in their original packaging and should not show signs of damage or heavy use. A valid purchase invoice or order confirmation is required when requesting a refund.
After the returned product passes inspection, the refund will be approved. Refunds are usually processed within five business days and are sent through the customer's original payment method. Shipping charges are generally not refundable unless the product was damaged or the wrong item was delivered.
If customers have questions regarding refunds, they can contact our customer support team for assistance.
