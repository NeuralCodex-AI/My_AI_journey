Warranty Policy
TechNova Store provides warranties for most electronic products. Gaming laptops include a two-year manufacturer warranty covering hardware defects. Smartphones include a one-year warranty, while wireless headphones and computer accessories include a six-month warranty.
The warranty covers manufacturing defects but does not include physical damage, water damage, accidental drops, or damage caused by unauthorized repairs. Customers should keep their purchase invoice because it is required when making a warranty claim.
If a product develops a manufacturing fault during the warranty period, customers should contact our support team. After inspection, the product may be repaired, replaced, or serviced according to the manufacturer's warranty policy.
