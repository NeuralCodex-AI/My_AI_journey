Frequently Asked Questions
Customers can place an order by selecting products, adding them to the shopping cart, and completing the checkout process. Orders can be cancelled within 24 hours before they are shipped. Once an order has been dispatched, cancellation is no longer possible.
Customers must create an account before placing an order. An account allows users to track orders, view previous purchases, and manage personal information.
If customers need help, they can contact our support team by email at support@technovastore.com or by phone during business hours. Most customer questions are answered within one business day. Our support team is happy to assist with product information, payments, shipping, refunds, and technical issues.
