Shipping Policy
TechNova Store provides both standard and express shipping services. Standard delivery usually takes between three and five business days, while express delivery normally arrives within one or two business days. International shipping is available to selected countries and usually takes between seven and fifteen business days.
After an order is shipped, customers receive a tracking number by email. The tracking number allows customers to monitor the delivery status of their package. Delivery times may be affected by weather conditions, holidays, or courier delays.
Customers should ensure that their shipping address and contact information are correct before placing an order to avoid delivery problems.
